Dreamy Apple Pie

Rich, decadent apple pie with a crumbly, crunchy topping is too good to pass up!

Yield

12 serving(s)

Prep Time

20 minutes

Cook Time

1 hour

Total Time

1 hr 20 minutes(s)

Pie Ingredients:

1 whole unbaked pie crust

3 whole large granny smith apples, peeled, cored, and sliced thin

1/2 cup brown sugar

1/2 cup sugar

1 trbsp alll-purpose Flour

1/2 cup heavy cream

2 tsp vanilla extract

1/8 tsp cinnamon

For the topping:

7 tbsp butter

3/4 cup all purpose flour

1/2 cup brown sugar

1/4 cup peccans

dash of salt

Directions

For the pie: Preheat oven to 375 degrees

Roll out pie dough and place it in a pie pan. Decorate the edges as desired

Add apple slices to a large bowl.In a spearate bowl, mix together cream, burown sugar, sugar, flour vanilla, and cinnamon. Pour over apples. Pour apples into tpie shell.

For the topping: In a bowl of a food processor, combine butter,, flour, sugar, pecans, and slat. Mix until evenything comes together in clumps. Pour topping over the apples.

Attach foil to the edges and lay a peice of flat foil loosely over the top of the pie. Place pie pan on top of a rimmed cookie sheet and bak for one hour. At the end, remove foil and allo to finish baking and browning. Can bak for up to 15 or 20 minutes longer if necessary.

Remove from oven when pie is bubbly and golden brown

Serve warm with hard sauce, whipped cream, or ice cream.
